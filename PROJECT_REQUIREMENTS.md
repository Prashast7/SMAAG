# SMAAG Website Design Prompt

## Project Overview
Create a clean, modern, and professional website for **SM Assurance & Advisory Group, APC (SMAAG)** — a California-based CPA firm. The website should feel authoritative, trustworthy, and forward-thinking without being flashy or overcomplicated. It is a solo CPA practice targeting small to mid-size businesses across all industries in the United States.

---

## Brand Identity

- **Full firm name:** SM Assurance & Advisory Group, APC
- **Brand abbreviation:** SMAAG
- **Tagline:** Assurance. Advisory. Delivered.
- **Website domain:** smaagcpa.com
- **Contact email:** shivangi@smaagcpa.com
- **Location:** San Francisco Bay Area, California
- **Primary color:** Deep teal
- **Secondary colors:** White and light grey
- **Font style:** Clean, modern, sans-serif — professional but not corporate-stiff
- **Overall feel:** Boutique firm with big firm credentials. Confident, minimal, credible.

## Design Guidelines

**Layout:**
- Single-column or two-column layout — clean and uncluttered
- Generous white space throughout
- No stock photos of people — use abstract or geometric design elements if visuals are needed
- Simple icons where relevant — not clip art

**Navigation:**
- Top navigation bar: Home | About | Services | Vision | Contact
- Mobile responsive — must work perfectly on phone
- Sticky header so navigation stays visible while scrolling

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep teal — use for headings, buttons, and accents
- Secondary: Light grey — use for section backgrounds, dividers, and subtle accents
- Background: White or very light grey
- Text: Dark charcoal — not pure black

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold, clean sans-serif — Inter, Poppins, or similar
- Body text: Regular weight, comfortable line spacing — easy to read
- Avoid decorative or script fonts

**Buttons:**
- Primary button: Deep teal background, white text
- Hover effect: Slightly darker teal
- Rounded corners — not sharp, not pill-shaped

**Footer:**
- Firm name: SM Assurance & Advisory Group, APC
- Tagline: Assurance. Advisory. Delivered.
- Email: shivangi@smaagcpa.com
- Location: San Francisco Bay Area, California
- Copyright: © 2026 SM Assurance & Advisory Group, APC. All rights reserved.
- Simple, one-row footer — no clutter

---

## What to Avoid

- No stock photos of people shaking hands or sitting in boardrooms
- No animated elements or flashy transitions
- No testimonials section — firm is new, no client quotes yet
- No pricing page
- No blog or news section
- No social media icons unless LinkedIn only
- No mention of specific software tools or third-party partners
- No mention of technology, AI, or future tech plans
- No tax services — not currently offered
- No fractional CFO services — not currently offered
- No overly complex layouts or sidebars
- Keep every page to the point — less is more

---

## Technical Requirements

- Mobile responsive — fully optimized for phone and tablet
- Fast loading — minimal heavy images or scripts
- SSL certificate enabled — https
- Inquiry form must actually send emails to shivangi@smaagcpa.com
- Pop-up must be closeable easily
- Contact page form and pop-up form submissions go to the same email
- Basic SEO — page titles, meta descriptions, alt text on images
- No login or member area needed
- No e-commerce functionality needed

---

## Tone of Voice Throughout

- Professional but approachable
- Confident without being arrogant
- Clear and direct — no jargon
- First person singular where referring to Shivangi — "I bring..." not "We bring..." on the About page since it is a solo practice
- First person plural "we" acceptable on Services and Vision pages to signal the firm identity
- No exclamation marks
- No buzzwords like "synergy", "holistic", "leverage" etc.

---
